About the Event

Shaping Boys into Men

The world is changing, and the need for positive male role models has never been greater. Through a structured curriculum led by mentors acting as a North Star, we shape boys into well-rounded, confident, and purpose-driven men.

Vision

To inspire boys to uncover their true identity and fulfill their God-given purpose.

Mission

To cultivate boys into men of character who positively influence society and raise future generations of strong, responsible men.
