Who We Are

A mentorship initiative built for this generation

Too many boys are growing up without positive male role models. The Genesis Initiative exists to change that recognising the vital role boys play in shaping the future and providing the essential guidance they need to thrive.

Through lessons on self-esteem, leadership, purpose, emotional intelligence, relationships, and more, Project Genesis shapes boys into well-rounded, confident, and responsible men who can impact their families, workplaces, communities, and culture.

Our Vision

To inspire boys to uncover their true identity and fulfill their purpose.

Our Mission

To cultivate boys into men of character who positively influence society and raise future generations of strong, responsible men.

Our Story

We Exist to Shape Boys Into Men Of Character

The Genesis Initiative was born from a simple but urgent truth: too many boys are navigating the most critical years of their lives without guidance, without models, without direction.

The Beginning

A generation in need of direction

The Genesis Initiative was designed in response to the growing lack of positive male role models in society. We recognised the vital role boys play in shaping the future — and the crisis created when they are left to navigate it alone.

"The most dangerous thing in society is a young man without a purpose."

Through lessons on self-esteem, leadership, purpose, emotional intelligence, relationships, and more, The Genesis Initiative shapes boys into well-rounded, confident, and responsible men. Men who can impact their families, workplaces, communities, and culture.

Boys aged 10–16 are paired with mentors who act as guiding North Stars helping them navigate life and discover their potential through a structured, five-pillar curriculum.

Two Ways to Serve

Find your place in the Genesis Initiative

Whether you're ready to commit to a one-on-one mentoring relationship or want to support our mission in a more flexible capacity, there is a meaningful role for you here.

Become a Mentor
Become a Volunteer

Become a Mentor

As a mentor, you commit to a structured one-on-one relationship with a boy aged 10–16, walking with him through our five-module curriculum and life itself. You become his North Star a steady, consistent presence he can look to for guidance, challenge, and encouragement.

Mentors meet with their mentees regularly, work through the Genesis curriculum together, and check in with our programme coordinators. This is a relationship of depth not convenience — and it is the most powerful thing you can offer a young man.

Through a structured curriculum, mentors empower boys to grow into confident, purpose-driven men who contribute meaningfully to society.

What We're Looking For
  • Male, aged 25 and above
  • Commitment of at least 12 months
  • Willingness to complete our mentor training programme
  • A heart for young people and a stable personal lifestyle
  • Availability to meet 2–4 hours per month
  • A genuine desire to shape the next generation

Become a Volunteer

Volunteers are the engine behind the Genesis Initiative. From event coordination and communications to programme support and community outreach — there's a wide range of ways to contribute, without the long-term commitment of mentorship.

Volunteers can serve on a project-by-project basis or commit to ongoing monthly support. No matter how much time you can offer, we will find the right fit for your skills and schedule.

Volunteer Opportunities
  • Event planning and coordination
  • Social media and content creation
  • Fundraising and donor relations
  • Workshop facilitation and guest speaking
  • Administrative and operational support
  • Photography, design, and communications
