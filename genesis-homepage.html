Who We Are

A mentorship initiative built for this generation

Too many boys are growing up without positive male role models. The Genesis Initiative exists to change that — pairing boys with mentors who act as a guiding "North Star," inspiring them to discover their identity and fulfill their purpose.

Through a structured curriculum covering character, entrepreneurship, emotional intelligence, and more, we help boys grow into confident, purpose-driven men who contribute meaningfully to society.

Character Development
